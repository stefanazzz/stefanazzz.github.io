Seismic refraction transect on a breached dyke, with Geophysics student field trip

Video of nucleating and dynamically propagating fracture on a sliding surface close to frictional instability. The video is obtained using a ultra-high speed camera and an interferometric optical technique, allowing to visualize the strain associated to the propagation of dynamic fracture. The horizontal size of the sample is 15 cm and the total duration of the movie is 0.74 milliseconds.

S. Nielsen et al. https://doi.org/10.1111/j.1365-246x.2009.04444.x
